I have been an RN since 2014, and I treated myself to this stethoscope as I am training for emergency/critical care. Prior to this I have used both Littman lightweight and Littman classic stethoscopes - the difference in acoustic quality in this cardiology stethoscope is huge. With my old stethoscopes I found it difficult to really assess my patients well when they had decreased breath sounds, and sometimes it was difficult to identify extra heart sounds or murmurs - everything is so much clearer with this one. The only con is that it is a bit bulky compared to the Littman classic, but this isn't a deal-breaker for me, the higher sound quality is worth it!
