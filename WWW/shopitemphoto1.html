Only had this a few days, but so far I am impressed. I wanted a small camera that I could carry at all times, with a descent optical zoom and not pay to much for. Picture quality is great for this size of camera and it is very easy to use. Don’t expect the quality to be on a par with a DSLR. But if like me you can not haul your DSLR equipment everywhere you go, it’s a great stop gap. Has some great settings on it to get some cool effects and the video quality is brilliant. I transfer pictures straight to my IPad using the Sony Playmobile app. Which means I can put pictures straight onto FB or send pictures to friends and family straight away. I have not had a chance to test all of the camera functions, but the ones I had needed have worked well. Might be an idea to purchase a spare battery/charger, as always handy when out and about.
