Love Calendar

Welcome to Love Calendar, your number one source for social circle personalised calendars. We are dedicated to giving you the very best presents suggestions based on your loved one’s hobbies and interests. Love Calendar aims to provide you with a snapshot of your relationship with each calendar recipient, where you will be able to check all the important dates, places, and memories that you shared. And the cherry on top of the cake is that by doing so, we will provide you with visual memory stimuli that will aim to improve your memory recall of said events.

HA HA Moment

While trying to come up with an idea for an app, that diverged from my usual game development route, I am ashamed to admit that I forgot two important dates that meant the world to my wife in a matter of days. With that comes the fact that I didn’t even have any presents to save the day, it was a complete right off that left me in the “dog house” for an unusually long time. It was at this moment that I thought that I needed to create a calendar app that would not just remind me of these important dates with plenty of time in advance, but that would also give me presents suggestions based on my wife interests, hobbies, profession, etc, to make this process as stress-free as possible, and that would help me improve my memory of any important dates that we share.

App Developement

With COVID spreading around the world it affected us all, and in my case, I consider myself one of the lucky ones, my daughter caught it and survived, from our social circles until now only some friends relatives died, that we had a remote connection, and since myself and my wife are essential workers, our work was safe, apart from an increased workload, and the sense that we needed to do whatever possible to help, what brings me back to app development, that it is important but was somewhat left in the back burner due to all of the above.
