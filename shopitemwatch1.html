The watch itself is elegant and as described, though I do find it rather a triumph of style over substance. In particular, the bezel doesn't rotate, the "sweep" hand is for seconds rather than a tachymetre function, and the auxiliary buttons (stop/pause/reset) are rather loose and a tad over sensitive. That said, the watch thus far keeps excellent time and has proved reliable. It is also very comfortable to wear - assuming, like me, you prefer a large and reasonably heavy watch. The stainless steel strap is easy to adjust and the supplied link removal tool is a nice touch.
